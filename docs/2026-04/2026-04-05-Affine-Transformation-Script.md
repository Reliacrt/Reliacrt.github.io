## Affine Transformation

Firstly, I will write down the definition of affine transformation. Specifically, $f$ denotes the affine transformation, $A$ denotes its linear transformation, $\mathbf{x}$ denotes a vector acted upon by the linear transformation $A$, and $\mathbf{b}$ signifies the offset applied after the linear transformation $A$.
$$
f(\mathbf{x})=A\mathbf{x}+\mathbf{b}
$$
